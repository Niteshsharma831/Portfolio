What do You Want to Know About Me

Hello! I'm Nitesh Kumar Sharma, a passionate UX/UI Designer, Web Developer, and App Developer currently pursuing a degree in Computer Engineering at RK University. With a strong foundation in web and app development, combined with a keen eye for design, I specialize in creating intuitive and visually appealing digital experiences.

I have a year's experience in UX/UI design, during which I have honed my skills using tools like Figma and Sketch. I enjoy crafting designs that not only look good but also solve user problems and enhance usability. Whether it's developing responsive websites or building robust mobile applications, I take pride in delivering high-quality, user-centered solutions.

Apart from my technical expertise, I also run a YouTube channel called 'Code & Create', where I share coding tutorials and design tips, helping others on their journey to becoming proficient developers and designers. My goal is to constantly grow and refine my skills while helping others along the way.

Feel free to explore my projects, and let’s connect if you'd like to collaborate!

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Engineering at RK University, where I am in the 5th semester of my 3rd year. Throughout my academic journey, I have developed strong technical skills in web and app development, programming, and design through hands-on experience in various projects and internships.

Before college, I completed my intermediate education at Mahavir Inter College, Gaya, Bihar, with a focus on the science stream and mathematics honors, achieving 72% marks. My interest in technology and problem-solving during my intermediate studies inspired me to pursue a career in computer engineering.

At university, I have further expanded my skill set in Flutter, JavaScript, C#, and PostgreSQL, and I’ve gained practical experience through real-world projects and a web development internship.
